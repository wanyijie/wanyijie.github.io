## Have Got
**Usage: have got**
>have got + noun = possess noun/have noun/own noun

**Grammer: have got**
>have => have/has
>I/You/We/They + have got + noun
>He/She/It + has got + noun

**Example: have got**
| subj. | have | got |  noun|
|-|-|-|-|
| I | have | got | a car. |
| You | have | got | a bike. |
|He | has | got | a laptop. |
|She | has | got | a dog. |
| It | has | got | 5 stars. |
| We | have | got | books. |
| They | have | got | pens. |

**Grammar: questions**
>HAVE => have/has
>(Q.+)have + I/you/we/they + got noun...?
>(Q.+)has + he/she/it + got + noun...?

**Examples: Questions >> Many Answers**
